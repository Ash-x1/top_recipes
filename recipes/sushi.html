Sushi

Image of the final result of Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish, and it's so popular among east asian food lovers because it's delicious taste and because the new experience that gives for non-asians, give it a try and you will fall in it's love immediately

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Nori sheets
  • Raw fish (e.g., salmon, tuna)
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

Steps:

  1. Rinse and cook the sushi rice.
  2. Season the rice with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  3. Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo mat.
  4. Spread rice evenly over the nori.
  5. Place fillings such as fish, cucumber, and avocado in the center.
  6. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat.
  7. Slice the roll into pieces.
  8. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
