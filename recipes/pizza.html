Pizza

Image of the final result of Pizza

Pizza is an italic dish, and it's so popular worldwide because it's delicious taste and because it's easy to prepare, give it a try and you will fall in it's love immediately

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives)

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil to form a dough.
  2. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rise until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the oven.
  5. Roll out the dough to the desired thickness.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Add mozzarella cheese and chosen toppings.
  8. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing.
