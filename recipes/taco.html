This is one of the most awesome dishes that you can ever experience, maybe u saw taco before in a show or someone mentioned it in front of you, however you must give it a shot!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Small tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps:

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in taco seasoning and water according to package instructions.
  4. Finely chop onions, tomatoes, and peppers.
  5. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  6. Warm tortillas in a skillet or microwave.
  7. Fill tortillas with beef mixture.
  8. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
